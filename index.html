Alpha Art Gallery Foundation

Established in 2012, the Alpha Art Gallery Foundation supports emerging artists across Lebanon and the region through grants, residencies, and public programs. Our mission is to make art accessible and foster sustainable careers for young creators.

  • Residencies: 3–6 month studio time with mentorship
  • Grants: Production and exhibition micro-funds
  • Education: Free talks, school visits, and workshops
